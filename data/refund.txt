Our refund policy allows customers to request a refund within 30 days of purchase. To be eligible, the customer must provide a valid receipt or proof of purchase.

Refunds are typically processed within 5 business days after approval. However, depending on the payment provider, it may take additional time for the funds to appear in the customer’s account.

In cases where a product is defective or damaged upon arrival, customers are entitled to a full refund or replacement. The issue must be reported within 7 days of delivery.

Partial refunds may be granted in specific situations, such as:
- items returned in a used condition
- items missing original packaging
- returns initiated after the standard 30-day window

Digital products are generally non-refundable unless there is a technical issue that prevents access or usage.

Refund requests must be submitted through the official support channels. Requests sent via social media or unofficial communication channels may not be processed.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product is defective or incorrect.

If a refund is approved, the original payment method will be used whenever possible. If this is not feasible, store credit may be issued instead.

Refunds are not available for promotional or discounted items unless explicitly stated.

In case of disputes, the company reserves the right to investigate the claim and request additional documentation.

Processing times may be longer during peak seasons such as holidays or promotional events.

Customers who repeatedly abuse the refund policy may have their refund privileges restricted.

For subscription services, refunds are only granted for the unused portion of the billing period, and only under exceptional circumstances.

If a refund request is denied, the customer will receive a detailed explanation outlining the reason for the decision.
