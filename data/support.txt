Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time. Support is not available on weekends or public holidays.

Customers can contact support via email, live chat, or through the help center portal. Response times may vary depending on the volume of requests.

Typical response time for email inquiries is between 24 to 48 hours. Live chat usually provides faster responses, often within minutes during working hours.

For urgent issues, customers are encouraged to use live chat rather than email.

Support requests should include as much detail as possible, including order number, screenshots, and a clear description of the issue.

Incomplete requests may lead to delays in response time.

Our support team handles a wide range of issues including:
- account access problems
- payment and billing inquiries
- refund requests
- technical issues with the platform

For technical issues, customers should provide device information, browser type, and steps to reproduce the problem.

The support team may escalate complex issues to specialized technical teams when necessary. This may increase resolution time.

Customers will receive a confirmation email after submitting a support request. This email includes a ticket ID for tracking purposes.

Follow-up messages should reference the original ticket ID to avoid duplication.

Support agents are trained to provide accurate and helpful responses, but response quality may vary depending on the complexity of the issue.

Abusive or inappropriate language towards support staff will not be tolerated and may result in restricted access to support services.

Support does not handle refund approvals directly; refund decisions are processed according to the refund policy.

During peak times, such as product launches or major updates, response times may be longer than usual.

Customers can also consult the FAQ section for common issues before contacting support.

The support team aims to resolve most issues within 72 hours, although some cases may take longer depending on complexity.

For enterprise customers, priority support options are available with dedicated response channels.

If a support request is resolved, the ticket will be marked as closed. Customers can reopen the ticket if the issue persists.
